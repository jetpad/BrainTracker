Reaction Time Tracker

This site lets you track your reaction speed over time. Reaction speed is an indicator of the health of your brain and by proxy, the health of your whole body. It can help you see how diet and lifestyle changes effect your health. Using this site requires a dropbox account. Your reaction time data is saved in a private location to your dropbox account but does not have access to any of your other dropbox files. Using dropbox lets you run tests from multiple devices and locations while the data is all saved together.
